# Jukebox Flat Audio — Fabric Mod for Minecraft 1.21.1

Disables 3D positional audio **only** for jukeboxes. All other sounds (footsteps, mobs, ambient, etc.) remain fully positional. A toggle is added to **Options → Music & Sounds**.

---

## What it does

| Situation | Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Toggle **ON** (default) | Jukebox music plays at full volume everywhere, no distance fade, no panning — just like background music |
| Toggle **OFF** | Vanilla behaviour restored — jukebox music is 3D and fades with distance |
